Our Commitment

THE MANIFESTO.

"ChronoX was born from a simple observation: the world's most interesting watches aren't made by conglomerates, but by independent visionaries who dare to experiment with mechanical art."

The Evolution

A DECADE OF
HOROLOGY.

2016 / The Start

The London Spark

Starting as a private collective of collectors in Shoreditch, we began documenting the rise of independent watchmakers who prioritized mechanical soul.

2020 / Expansion

The Digital Atelier

ChronoX transitioned into a curated marketplace, providing a platform for microbrands to reach a global audience of enthusiasts who value technical transparency.

2026 / Present

Global Curation

Today, we represent over 50 brands across 15 countries, maintaining the same standard of mechanical excellence and artisanal integrity we started with.
